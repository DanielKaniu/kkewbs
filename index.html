About

Who we are

Started in 2005 for K-Krew Gospel artists to come and study the Word of God together at Mamlaka Hill Chapel, Nairobi. In 2013, We moved to Nairobi Cinema In the CBD Due to the growing number of members both gospel artist, Djs, Mcs, Dancers, Ministers, and young adults with diverse talents using them to reach out and spread the good news of Christ. For better interaction, we further have small groups within referred to as Counties,Namely:

  • Timesis county
  • 2nd Chance county
  • The Voice county
  • OG county
  • Wow county
  • Indomitables county
  • Mwangaza county
  • Enlighters county
  • Dance county
  • Iquest county
  • Transformers county

We meet every Wednesday from 5.30 pm to 8.00pm at Nairobi Cinema. You are invited to join us and meet great Christian friends and family Totally Sold out for Christ.

Services

What we do

Bible Study

We share the word together every Wednesday; starting 5 p.m. @ Nairobi Cinema.

Hood Ministry

We have a team that is involved in the evangelism of the good news of Christ, in the hood.

Each One Reach One #EORO

On Saturdays, we converge at a designated place and send each other on a mission to share God's word to the people who are around that place. More like a one-on-one evangelism.

Goals for Christ

We have a football club that shares God's word when they meet to use their talents.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When is the weekly Bible study?

    Our Bible study happens every Wednesday at Nairobi Cinema (5:30 p.m. to 8 p.m.)

  • How does someone join a small group (county)?

    Come for fellowship on Wednesday evening and you will be guided how to join one.

  • Can anyone join a ministry of his/her liking?

    Yes, our departments are open to new ministers. However, you must show some committment, because this is God's work and it needs some seriousness.
